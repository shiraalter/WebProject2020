About Us


Our story begins in a small basement in Iron River, Michigan in the Winter of 1920. After immigrating to America from Sweden, the Karlsson siblings decided to chase after the American Dream by doing what they loved: brewing well-crafted beer. Soon after launching their small family-owned business with little money, the company flourished and became a national sensation.

The business has expanded over many generations and we are now a proud household name. As a family-owned business, we stand by the top-notch quality of our products and are determined to continue the family legacy with integrity.

Fighting Fish Brewery FAQs


Can I create my own flavors?

YES! We are known for crafting the most original and unique beer flavors according to each customer's liking. You can also purchase our more classic flavors which never disappoint!

How long does shipping take?

Ground shipping typically takes between 3-5 business days in the US.

Can I get crafted beer at my events?

Absolutely! We have catered birthday parties, weddings, super bowl parties, fundraisers and many more. Feel free to send us an email with any inquiries regarding a specific event.
